Hugo-defined: automatic summary split

By default, Hugo automatically takes the first 70 words of your content as its summary and stores it into the .Summary variable, which you may use in your templates.

Pros: Automatic, no additional work on your part. Cons: All HTML tags are stripped from the summary, and the first 70 words, whether they belong to a heading or to different paragraphs, are all lumped into one paragraph.
